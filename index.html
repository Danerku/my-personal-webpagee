Who We Are

Welcome to the Economics Policy and Innovation Centre for Health Systems – EPIC Health Systems. We are a Research and Training Institute that specializes in strengthening health systems in low- and middle-income countries. Through our innovative partnerships with governments, health organizations, the private sector, and higher education institutions, EPIC Health Systems offers strategies, tools, management, and training support needed to create high-functioning, accountable, affordable, accessible, and reliable health systems.

Our work is driven by a commitment to address key health challenges, achieve universal health coverage, enhance health security, and improve health system resilience, ultimately improving population health. We enhance health system functions through policy and practice changes that expand access, coverage, quality, and efficiency of health services, resulting in better health outcomes in the countries and regions we serve.

Mission, Vision, and Values

Our Mission

To work with partners—including governments, health organizations, funders, communities, and the private sector—to build high-functioning, resilient, and sustainable health systems in Africa that are accessible, affordable, and accountable, thereby transforming health outcomes for the continent's most vulnerable populations.

Core Values

  • Innovation: We strive for continuous improvement and creative solutions that challenge conventional approaches and advance the field of health systems strengthening.
  • Collaboration: We believe in the power of partnership and actively engage with governments, universities, organizations, and communities to build inclusive and effective health systems.
  • Integrity: We conduct our work with the highest ethical standards, ensuring transparency, accountability, and fairness in all our interactions.
  • Respect: We honor the diversity and expertise of the communities and partners we work with, valuing their insights and prioritizing their needs in our efforts.
  • Excellence: We are committed to delivering the highest quality of research and training driven by data and evidenced-based practices to achieve the best possible outcomes.
